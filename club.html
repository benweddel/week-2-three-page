Becoming a Member

Membership Information

Anyone can join. You don't even have to have picked up a paddle before. The annual fee for an adult member is $280 for unlimited play for the entire year, (September – August) and includes full membership to the APTA. Family membership is $530. Participation in league play has a modest additional fee to cover the cost of balls. Annual junior membership (under 19) is $110 and for those between 19-24, $150. The initiation fee for 19-24 year olds is $190 and for adults, $230. Initiation fees can be paid over a five year period. There is simply no better value for your sporting dollar than this!   Register Now

Leagues

House Leagues – Men’s & Women’s

Participation in the leagues is the best way to learn and improve at the sport as well as to meet other members. The format of our leagues allows all levels of players to take part in competitive matches in a fun and social atmosphere. Nearly all our members play in at least one league. These run from September to April and are comprised of four sessions, each running about 8 weeks.

Men’s Leagues

Tuesday Evenings

Tuesday night play brings together players of roughly the same ability into groups of 8-12 players. Players partner up with a different group member in each of 3 sets, and all the games a player wins over the course of the session are tallied up at the end. Players with the top scores move to a higher group and those with low scores move to a lower group.

Wednesday Evenings

On Wednesday night, players play with the same partner for the entire 8 week session. On Wednesday night players of all abilities play together, creating a great opportunity for members to practice and learn from more experienced players. Also, because no formal scores are kept, Wednesday nights are very social.

Women’s Leagues

Monday Evenings

Players partner up with a different person in each of 3 sets, and all the games a player wins over the course of the session are tallied up at the end. Players with the top scores move to a higher group and those with the low scores move to a lower group. Entry and intermediate level players play Monday night.

Thursday Evenings

Has the same format as Monday's but more advanced players play Thursday evening.
